SAFE-SIGHT

Safe-Sight is an intelligent safety system designed for vans, trucks, and large vehicles. It detects vehicles or pedestrians in blind spot areas and immediately alerts the driver through voice notifications to prevent accidents. With built-in GPS tracking, Safe-Sight also allows real-time vehicle monitoring for better safety and fleet management.
